Gis Analysis

GIS Analysis is a system designed to capture,store,manipulate,manage and represent all forms of geographical data.At ARDMs we conduct GIS Analysis Using -ArcGIS,QGIS,Global Mapper and Google Earth,the end product usually depends on the client from thematic maps,topo maps, pie charts,bar graphs etc

Land Surveying

land surveying is the technique, profession, art and science of determining the terrestrial or positions of points and the distances and angles between them At ARDEMs land surveying is done using the latest survey equiupment Total Stations,RTKS,Dumpy or electronic level Survey techniques including static survey is done with highly qualified proffesionals

Topographic Survey

Topographic survey is the technique of collection ground data (x,y,z) and representing this information on a piece paper,the elevation values are represented using contour lines and spot heights. The aim of a topographic survey is to collect data on elevation and every man-made and natural feature with the end product being a map.At ARDEMs data is collected using the latest survey equpment and analysed using the latest software
